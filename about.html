The Intelligent Data Analytics and Systems Lab (IDAS) was established by Prof. Guoqiang Zhong in the Department of Information Science and Engineering of Ocean University of China (OUC), and is one of the earliest laboratories in the field of deep learning established by OUC in recent years. The main research areas are artificial intelligence, machine learning, pattern recognition, computer vision, big data analysis, robotics, and related technology applications.

The Intelligent Data Analysis and Systems Laboratory will gather professional scholars in the field of artificial intelligence to conduct pioneering and forward-looking research and promote the research of the Department of Information Science and Engineering of Ocean University of China in the field of artificial intelligence.

Through a series of institutional innovations, IDAS will create an environment for daily communication and deep integration of researchers from multiple fields, establish a first-class core experimental platform and an all-English working environment, and systematically attract domestic and foreign scholars in the field of AI to work at Ocean University of China. At the same time, through the dual engine of university and enterprise innovation, it breaks through the core AI technology, cultivates leading talents in the intelligent industry, and promotes the leapfrog development of the intelligent industry.
